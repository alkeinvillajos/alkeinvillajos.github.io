About Me

Thank you for visiting my realm in this digital sphere. I live in Tangub City, Misamis Occidental all the way from Mindanao but fly away to Cebu City to take everything that the city has to offer.

Aside from being a musician and a photographer, I wanted a site where I could showcase my technical skills that I have acquired, skills that I'm working on and skills that I'm hoping to obtain.

I have always loved the web and computer science, but it has not been until recently that I decided to fully move ahead to share what I know and wish to learn with the world. From basic HTML and CSS to programming in C and C#, I'm looking to build on what I do know and learn what I do not - or at least obtain a basic understanding in order to have an intelligent conversation.

This site was created from scratch without the use of a CMS, themes or builders. I use WordPress often, but here I wanted to display that I could design and develop a site from the ground up and for now I am more on Front-End Development and started my basics in Back-End Development.

So here you will catch a glimpse of my skills in action and also get a sneak peek on what skills are to come.

I'm always glad to help so do not hesistate to contact me!
